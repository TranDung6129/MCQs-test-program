Welcome to MCQs test program!

There are some instruction for you to create your own test

1) If you already have a test, and you want to continue add question, click the file button and "Open..." your file
2) Enter your question, choices, answer to reference textbox.
3) Mind that in choices box, each choice must seperate from other choices by "| ". Example: choice1, choice2, choice3,...
4) Same as answer box, each answer must separate from other answers by "| "
3) After enter all the field, you click "Create" button to add new question to your test.
4) If you add all your question, click File in menu and click "Save" to save your file.
5) If in the adding process you made some fault, don't worry and click "Edit" in menu to fix this fault.
